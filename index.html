About Us

  • 2017-2018

    2017 was the founding year of the squadron as well as the drill team. Luckily it got off to an amazing start, over 50 cadets showed up to the tryouts! Allowing us to have a strathcona tream right off the bat. The team worked amazingly together, creating an expetional silent sequence and winning their very first regional competition!

  • 2018-2019

    The second year of the drill team went just as well or maybe even better than the first! Although it got off to a slow start, having less cadets show up to the tryouts, we were still able to put together a stong team. With the high team morale and cooperation, the team went on to win the regional competition for the second year in a row. In addition they moved on to provincials and placed 1st!

  • 2019-2020

    The turnout of last years tryouts was astounding! Again we were able to have a strathcona team as well as 5-10 cadets as spares. The silent sequence had just been finished being created when unfortunatley everything was cancelled due to the pandemic.

Covid-19 Updates

During normal conditions our practices would run every Monday from 18:00 - 21:00 hours. However,as you know, due to the global pandemic, many things have either shut down completly or become virutal. Luckily the 75 Drill Team is one that has become virtual. Although the end of the 2019-2020 season was entirely cancelled, we have started up an online team for the 2020-2021 year. It will be run by Warrant Officer Second Class Affricot. Weekly videos will be sent out and posted on the squadrn facebook page for those interested. There will be videos on basic drill movements as well as how to properly wear your uniform. Even if you are not interested in joining the acutal team, it would be beneficial for you to watch these videos, they will help you improve overall.

Fun Activities

One of the biggest myths about drill is that it is formal and boring all the time. This is not true however! At 75 we make drill exciting by having engaging activities. Some of these activities include having pizza parties, dressing up before competition and the team has even been painballing together. These are all great ways for the team to come together, socialize and boost morale. Team bonding is essential as the closer the team is, the more seamless their drill routine will be.

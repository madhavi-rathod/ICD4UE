Meet the Coaches

Officer Cadet West

OCdt West has been a part of 75 squadron as well as a drill team coach for as long as it has been running. She is a former cadet who was also part of the drill team during her cadet years. OCdt West has always had a strong passion for drill, she loves doing it as well as coaching the team. She is very experienced and has lead the 75 Drill Team to win two regional championships as well as one provincial championship. Officer Cadet West is easy to approach if you have any questions regarding the team or drill in general!

Civilllian Instructor Pitman

CI Pitman joined the squadron as well as the 75 Drill Team coaching staff in 2018. She was also part of her squadrons drill team back when she was a cadet. CI Pitman has been an excellent addition to the coaching staff, giving fresh new ideas for the silent sequence as well as motivating the team. She is always open to talk about drill or cadets in general as she was the Warrant Officer First Class of her squadron.

Master Seamen Ruttan

MS Ruttan is the newest member of the coaching staff. He joined the squadron in 2018 but decided to start assisting in the coaching of the drill team in 2019. An interesting thing about MS Ruttan is that he was not an air cadet but rather an army cadet when he was younger. He was still part of the drill team and participated in many nation wide competitions. Having Maser Seamen Ruttan as a part of the drill team staff is a huge asset as he brings more than just the basic air cadet drill skills to the table.

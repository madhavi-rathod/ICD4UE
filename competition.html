Regionals

The Ottawa Regional Drill Competition is generally held at Hangar 11 near the Ottawa International Airport. Strathcona, walsh and improv teams from all over the Ottawa region participate in this competition. First, the teams uniforms are inspected by one or two military personel who are well trained in drill, dress and deportment. Then their drill sequence(s) are judged by another group of military personel. Finally during closing parade, the teams are awarded and some are selected to move on to the provincial competition.



Provincials

The provincial competition consists of the top three ranking teams from the Ottawa and Quebec regions. This competition is usually held in St Jean Quebec. It runs very similar to the regional competition, however there are more judges and more pressure! The 75 drill team typically travels to the competition in a coach bus, wearing formal dress and later changing into cerimonial uniform. The team spends the whole day there, watching other sequences as well as presenting theirs. It is a very exciting competition!
